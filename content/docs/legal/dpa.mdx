## IT IS AGREED AS FOLLOWS

### 1. Definitions and Interpretation

#### 1.1 Definitions

Unless otherwise defined herein, capitalized terms and expressions used in this Agreement shall have the following meaning:

* **"Agreement"** means this Data Processing Agreement and all Annexes.
* **"Company Personal Data"** means any Personal Data relating to Company's end users provided to or Processed by the Processor on behalf of the Company pursuant to or in connection with the Principal Agreement.
* **"Data Protection Laws"** means all applicable laws relating to Processing of Personal Data and privacy that may exist in any relevant jurisdiction, including European Data Protection Laws and US Data Protection Laws.
* **"EEA"** means the European Economic Area.
* **"EU Personal Data"** means the Processing of Personal Data by the Processor to which data protection legislation of the European Union, or of a Member State of the European Union or EEA, applies.
* **"European Data Protection Laws"** means the GDPR, UK Data Protection Act 2018, the UK GDPR, ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC, FADP, and any associated or additional legislation in force in the EU, EEA, Member States of the European Union, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom as amended, replaced or superseded from time to time.
* **"FADP"** means the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection and its Ordinances, as amended from time to time.
* **"FDPIC"** means the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner.
* **"GDPR"** means General Data Protection Regulation EU2016/679.
* **"UK GDPR"** means General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 as applicable as part of UK domestic law.
* **"US Data Protection Laws"** includes the CCPA, CPA, CTDPA, UCPA, VACDPA, and any binding regulations promulgated thereunder, as amended or updated from time to time.
* **"Protected Area"** includes the EU, UK, and Switzerland, and territories with adequacy decisions.
* **"Personal Data"** means any information provided by Company to Processor that is protected under Data Protection Laws.
* **"Services"** means the product and data analytics services provided by the Processor.
* **"Subprocessor"** means any person appointed by or on behalf of Processor to Process Personal Data.
* **"Standard Contractual Clauses"** means:

  * UK Standard Contractual Clauses
  * EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  * Swiss adaptations of EU SCCs

#### 1.2 Other Terms

The terms "Controller", "Data Subject", "Member State", "Personal Data Breach", "Processing", and "Supervisory Authority" have the meanings in GDPR and UK GDPR. Under US laws, analogous terms such as "Consumer" may apply.

#### 1.3 CCPA Terms

The terms "sell", "sale", "share", "sharing", and "Service Provider" have the same meanings as in the CCPA.

### 2. Processing of Company Personal Data

#### 2.1 Company Obligations

* Ensure all Company Personal Data is collected, processed, transferred, and used in compliance with Data Protection Laws.
* Obtain all necessary authorizations and consents from Data Subjects.
* Instruct Processor to process Company Personal Data to provide Services, including AI Features if enabled.

#### 2.2 Processor Obligations

* Comply with all applicable Data Protection Laws.
* Only process Company Personal Data on Company's documented instructions.
* Notify the Company if instructions infringe Data Protection Laws.
* Not sell or share any Personal Data.

#### 2.3–2.7 Additional Provisions

* Annex I A sets out subject matter, duration, and categories of data.
* Processor acknowledges Service Provider obligations.
* Processor shall not retain, use, or disclose Personal Data beyond agreed scope.
* Company may audit Processor or terminate Agreement for violations.

---

### 3. Processor Personnel & Confidentiality

Processor must ensure personnel with access to Company Personal Data are reliable and bound by confidentiality obligations.

### 4. Security

Processor must implement technical and organizational security measures in line with GDPR/UK GDPR Article 32. Measures are detailed in Annex II.

### 5. Subprocessing

* Company authorizes listed Subprocessors (Annex III).
* Processor remains fully liable for Subprocessors.
* Company may object to Subprocessors with notice.

### 6. Data Subject Rights and Cooperation

Processor shall:

* Assist Company in fulfilling Data Subject rights.
* Notify Company of Data Subject requests.
* Provide information for impact assessments and DPIAs.

---

### 7. Personal Data Breach

Processor shall notify Company without undue delay upon becoming aware of a breach, and cooperate in mitigation.

---

### 8. Audits

Processor shall provide necessary information and allow Company audits to assess compliance.

---

### 9. Deletion or Return of Data

Upon termination, Processor shall securely return or delete Company Personal Data unless retention is legally required.
