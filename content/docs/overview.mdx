## What is Landing Analytics?

Landing Analytics began as an internal tool to ingest events and surface potential purchase intent. We decided to make this tool available to the market.

## How It Works

Our platform works by adding a lightweight tracking script to your landing page. This script sends anonymous usage data to our servers, where it's processed, analyzed, and displayed on our intuitive dashboard.

### Getting started is simple:

1. **Sign up** for a free account
2. **Create a new project** in your dashboard
3. **Copy the JavaScript snippet** provided
4. **Paste it into your HTML page**
5. **That's it!** You're now ready to start collecting insights
