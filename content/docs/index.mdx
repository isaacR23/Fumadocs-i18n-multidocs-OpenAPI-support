## Overview
Landing Analytics help you how you landing page impact your business. It is all about **content** and we are on a missions to transform raw information into actionable insights. 
You can sign up for free today and start collecting data from day zero.

## Key Features

- **Privacy-focused analytics** - we don't use cookies!
- **Easy integration** with popular frameworks
- **Real-time data and insights** for immediate feedback
- **A/B testing** with different landing page variants
- **Event tracking** for sign-ups, transactions, and customer actions
- **Section tracking** to understand user engagement
- **Conversion tracking** to measure business impact
