## E-commerce Analytics

Track user behavior, conversion rates, and sales performance across your online store. Understand customer journeys and optimize your sales funnel with detailed insights.

- Shopping cart analysis
- Product performance tracking
- Customer journey mapping
- Conversion optimization

## SaaS Applications

Monitor user engagement, feature adoption, and subscription metrics for your SaaS platform. Get insights into user behavior and improve retention.

- Feature usage tracking
- User engagement metrics
- Subscription analytics
- Churn prediction

## Content Platforms

Analyze content performance and user engagement for blogs, media sites, and content platforms. Understand what content resonates with your audience.

- Content engagement metrics
- Reader behavior analysis
- Popular content tracking
- Audience segmentation

## Small Side Projects

Perfect for indie developers and hobbyists looking to understand user behavior and growth in their personal projects. Get meaningful insights without complex setups.

- Easy implementation
- Cost-effective tracking
- Basic user analytics
- Growth monitoring

## Copywriters

Track the performance of your copy and content across different platforms. Understand how readers interact with your writing and optimize for better engagement.

- Copy performance metrics
- Reading time analysis
- Engagement tracking
- A/B testing for headlines
